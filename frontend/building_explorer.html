Populated building — ergonomic layout at scale

This viewer presents the populated building produced by the layout engine at full scale, with every room furnished according to its type using the ergonomic rule packs (Neufert, Panero-Zelnik, and ADA-derived clearances and aisles). It illustrates how the single-object photo-to-3D pipeline composes into whole-room and whole-building furnishing, the focus of Part 2 of the project.

Why it exists & what it documents: We wanted to show the ergonomic layout holding at full building scale across many rooms at once. It documents the Part-2 goal and the rule-pack-driven population that achieves it.
