What this shows: six room types populated at dense, denser and densest levels with item combinations a human would choose — following activity-based zoning (the modern-workplace standard: focus, collaboration and social micro-environments), the relational rules (chairs face their desks, the projector throws onto its screen, the bin sits at arm's reach, the coat rack flanks the door) and the full German ASR envelope (movement areas, route widths, legal staffing caps). Left = the solver's 2D floor plan with green clearance halos; right = the 3D render. Densities that would violate a clearance are trimmed by the capacity guard — never forced.
