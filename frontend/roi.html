📊 ROI — how much designer time & money SCS Studio saves

The use case: a designer/BIM planner furnishing buildings places every object by hand and must keep German ASR workplace rules satisfied — thousands of decisions per building. SCS Studio replaces the placement + compliance pass with a one-click solver run; the human keeps a short review/polish pass.

Assumptions: manual placement 2.0 min/object (industry 1.5–3.0: search, drag, rotate, clearance check) + a manual compliance re-check at 25% of placement time. Machine times are measured fleet runs; a human review pass is included in the machine column. Rates: German interior/BIM planner €45 / €60 / €90 per hour.

Annual planning example: a firm furnishing 20 mid-size buildings/yr saves ≈ 1,360 designer-hours ≈ €82,000 / yr at €60/h (€61k at €45, €122k at €90).
