Surface smoothing tradeoff (still images)

A still-image version of the smoothing study for side-by-side reading: the raw reconstruction and the mesh after 16 and after 40 Taubin iterations. It makes the same point without requiring WebGL — surface noise is reduced by smoothing, but the returns diminish quickly against a fixed face budget, so smoothing is a secondary lever behind model choice and polygon allocation.

Why it exists & what it documents: We needed a WebGL-free version of the smoothing study so the finding is legible in the documentation and to reviewers without a 3D viewer. It records the same diminishing-returns conclusion.
