Whole-building ergonomic population (result)

This viewer shows the building after automated furniture population: catalog and generated furniture placed across its rooms by the constraint-based layout engine, which respects per-category clearances, wall affinity, functional groupings (such as a chair in front of each desk), and the building's own fixed obstacles. It demonstrates the end goal of the pipeline — turning an empty architectural model into a furnished, IFC-exportable building.

Why it exists & what it documents: We needed to demonstrate the end-to-end outcome — an empty building turned into a furnished, IFC-exportable model by the layout engine. It documents that the single-object pipeline composes into whole-building furnishing.
