Optimization gains at a glance

This figure charts the face-count and file-size reductions achieved by the optimization pipeline across the furniture library, giving a visual summary of the quantitative results. The consistent, large reductions illustrate that the pipeline reliably compresses raw single-photo reconstructions into lightweight assets suitable for IFC delivery, independent of the original object's complexity.

Why it exists & what it documents: We wanted the optimization results legible at a glance rather than as a table. It documents, visually, that the reductions are large and consistent across the library.
