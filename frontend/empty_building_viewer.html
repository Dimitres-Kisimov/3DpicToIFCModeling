The architectural input: an empty building shell

This viewer shows the empty Duplex architectural model that the room-population stage furnishes: 21 rooms, 113 walls, 14 doors, and 24 windows loaded from a real IFC. It establishes the spatial context and the fixed constraints (walls, openings, and building elements such as columns) against which furniture must be placed, and is the input to the ergonomic layout engine.

Why it exists & what it documents: We had to establish the real architectural input and its fixed constraints before any furniture is placed. It documents the starting point of the room-population stage and the obstacles (walls, openings, columns) the layout engine must respect.
