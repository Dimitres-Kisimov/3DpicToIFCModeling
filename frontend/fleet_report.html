How to read the connections — each placed piece can carry a machine-checked link to its partner: in_front_of = chair tucked at its desk, facing it · on_top_of = monitor/laptop/lamp on the work surface, screen turned to the chair · beside = waste bin at arm's reach off the desk's side (0.10 m) · ring_around = stools/chairs fanned around their table · throws_onto = ceiling projector aimed at its screen at throw distance · audience_row_facing = presentation rows centred on and facing the display · door_flank = coat rack (and bin, opposite side) at the wall beside the door · mounted_on = display on the front wall · faces = lectern toward the audience.
