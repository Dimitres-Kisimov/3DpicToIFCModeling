The reconstruction-to-IFC tradeoff across all furniture categories

Extending the single-chair comparison, this figure lets the reader step through all ten furniture categories (bed, bookshelf, cabinet, chair, desk, lamp, office chair, sofa, stool, table) and inspect, for each, four representations: the raw generated mesh, the IFC-optimized mesh (optimize_ifc), a clean catalog reference, and the mesh-optimized GLB (clean_and_optimize). It demonstrates that the same optimization and catalog-versus-generated tradeoffs hold across the whole furniture universe, not only for chairs, and that a uniform optimization budget produces consistent, BIM-ready assets regardless of category.

Why it exists & what it documents: A claim about chairs is not a claim about furniture, so we generalised the four-way across all ten categories to confirm the optimization and catalog tradeoffs are not chair-specific. It documents that the pipeline behaves consistently across the whole furniture library.
